About Me

Hi, I'm Ernest, a web developer and designer with over a year of experience building responsive, and user-friendly websites. I focus on front-end development and enjoy creating clean, accessible interfaces that work well on any device.

Outside of work, I have a huge love for nature and animals. Being outdoors or around animals is something that sparks my creativity and helps keep me in perspective. I try to put that same balance and passion into the websites I build, making them not only appealing but also informative and user-friendly.

I keep learning new tools and techniques so I can keep improving. It’s almost impossible to have one or all of the tools in one’s skills on every site, and the tech is constantly evolving. At the end of the day, I aim at real problem-solution digital experiences with a positive impact.
